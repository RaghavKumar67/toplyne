Your campaigns have a blind spot.

Only 1-2% of your website visitors convert.

Your Google and Meta audiences and conversion events are learning from only 1-2% of your website traffic.

But many of the remaining 98% have high intent.

5-10% of your visitors delay their purchase, go to a competitor, or buy from marketplaces.

This is your Dark Funnel.

In here hide users and signals that Meta & Google desperately need. To identify your most valuable customers and to find more like them.
